VIOLETTA

✨ Information about Violetta ✨

Violetta is a Disney Channel telenovela from Latin America about a musically talented but lonely teenager who returns to her native Buenos Aires after living in Europe. While trying to hide her passion from her overprotective father, she discovers her musical talents, finds true friends, and navigates love and the challenges of growing up. .
